About Luminary

Born from the belief that great design changes everything.

"We started Luminary because we were tired of beautiful work that didn't move the needle — and effective work that forgot to be beautiful."

Founded in 2021 by three creatives who met at a design residency in Lisbon, Luminary has grown from a scrappy three-person studio into a 24-strong team with clients across four continents.

We live at the intersection of craft and technology. Every project is approached as an opportunity to push the boundaries of what's possible while staying deeply connected to what matters most — the human on the other side of the screen.

Our work spans brand identities, campaign systems, interactive platforms, and editorial design. What ties it all together is a relentless curiosity and an obsession with the details most studios overlook.
